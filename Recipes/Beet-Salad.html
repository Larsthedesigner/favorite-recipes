Beet Salad

Description and Ingredients

This beet salad with spinach, arugula, and goat cheese is a vibrant and flavorful dish that's as visually appealing as it is delicious. Tender baby spinach and peppery arugula provide a nutritious and flavorful base, while sliced cooked beets add earthy sweetness and vibrant color to the salad. Creamy and tangy goat cheese crumbles complement the sweetness of the beets, while toasted walnuts or pecans offer a satisfying crunch. Drizzled with a homemade balsamic vinaigrette dressing, this salad is bursting with freshness and flavor, making it a perfect addition to any meal or a light and healthy lunch option on its own.

Main Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized beets, cooked, peeled, and sliced
  • 4 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 2 cups arugula leaves
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup toasted walnuts or pecans (optional)

Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the balsamic vinaigrette dressing. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar, extra virgin olive oil, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, and pepper until well combined. Set aside.
  2. Place the baby spinach leaves and arugula leaves in a large salad bowl or on a serving platter, creating a bed of greens.
  3. Arrange the sliced cooked beets on top of the bed of greens.
  4. Sprinkle crumbled goat cheese evenly over the salad.
  5. If using, scatter the toasted walnuts or pecans on top of the salad for added crunch and flavor.
  6. Drizzle the prepared balsamic vinaigrette dressing over the salad just before serving, or serve the dressing on the side for individual drizzling.
  7. Toss the salad gently to combine all the ingredients and evenly coat them with the dressing.
  8. Serve the beet salad immediately as a refreshing and nutritious appetizer or side dish.
