Olivia's Signature Stirfry

Description and Ingredients

This tofu stir-fry with coconut aminos is a delicious and nutritious dish that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. The tofu is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, while the colorful array of vegetables adds freshness and crunch. The stir-fry sauce, made with coconut aminos, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger, infuses the dish with savory and aromatic flavors. Serve this stir-fry over a bed of rice or noodles for a complete and filling meal that's sure to please your taste buds. It's easy to make, packed with plant-based protein, and suitable for vegans and vegetarians alike.

  • 1 block (14 oz) firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas, trimmed
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (for stir-frying)
  • Cooked rice or noodles, for serving
  • Sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Start by preparing the tofu. Cut the pressed tofu into cubes or slices, depending on your preference.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the coconut aminos, sesame oil, minced garlic, and grated ginger to make the stir-fry sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the tofu cubes/slices in a single layer and cook until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the tofu from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add a bit more vegetable oil if needed. Add the sliced bell peppers, broccoli florets, julienned carrot, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the cooked tofu to the skillet with the vegetables. Pour the prepared stir-fry sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Toss everything together until well coated in the sauce.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly and the flavors to meld together.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
  8. Serve the tofu stir-fry hot over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy!
