Sardine Salad

Description and Ingredients

A description of Sardine Salad

  • Bunch of Kale
  • Half Lemon
  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • White Northern Beans
  • Feta
  • Can of Sardines (Boneless)

Instructions

  1. Pull Kale leafs off of stems
  2. Place Kale leafs into salad spinner and wash thuroughly
  3. Place washed Kale into mixing bowl and juice lemon onto Kale, add a pinch of salt, and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  4. Open can of white northn beans and wash throughouyl with strainer
  5. mix together beans and kale
  6. Add feta to top of salad
  7. Open can of sardines and strain out liquid
  8. Serve salad onto two plates and place Sardines on top
