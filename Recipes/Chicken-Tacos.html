Chicken Tacos

Description and Ingredients

These chicken tacos are a delicious and satisfying meal made with tender, flavorful chicken cooked low and slow in bone broth. The slow cooker does all the work, infusing the chicken with rich and savory flavors from the broth and aromatic spices like chili powder, cumin, and paprika. Serving the chicken on warm corn tortillas adds a deliciously authentic touch, while a variety of fresh toppings allows everyone to customize their tacos to their liking. Whether it's for a weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends and family, these chicken tacos are sure to be a hit!

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 2 cups bone broth (chicken or vegetable)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas
  • Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced avocado, salsa, sour cream, lime wedges, cilantro

Instructions

  1. Place the boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the bone broth, chopped onion, minced garlic, chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture over the chicken in the slow cooker.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  4. Once the chicken is cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, allowing it to absorb the flavorful broth.
  5. Warm the corn tortillas either on a dry skillet or in the microwave.
  6. To assemble the tacos, spoon some of the shredded chicken onto each warm corn tortilla.
  7. Add your favorite toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced avocado, salsa, sour cream, a squeeze of lime juice, and fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve the chicken tacos immediately and enjoy!
